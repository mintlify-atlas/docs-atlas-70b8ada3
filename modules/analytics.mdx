The Analytics module gives every tenant a live view of their business performance across four dimensions: commercial pipeline health, operational throughput, financial health, and per-job profitability. All dashboards are tenant-isolated via Row Level Security and composed from modular, configurable widgets backed by the `dashboards`, `dashboard_widgets`, `kpi_definitions`, and `kpi_history` tables introduced in migration `20260617000015_dashboards_core.sql`.

<Tip>
  Use the `?tenant=<code>` query parameter in your browser to switch between tenants in the demo environment. Each tenant's dashboard loads its own KPI history, widget layout, and branding without any re-login required.
</Tip>

## Dashboard Modules

### 1. Commercial Dashboard

Tracks the health and velocity of the sales pipeline from lead generation to deal close.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Pipeline Value by Stage" icon="funnel">
    Total estimated value of all open requirements at each CRM stage — Nuevo, En Contacto, Calificado, Propuesta, Negociación.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Conversion Rates" icon="arrow-trend-up">
    Stage-to-stage conversion percentages. Highlights where prospects drop out of the funnel.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Quote Win/Loss Ratio" icon="scale-balanced">
    Percentage of issued proposals that closed as Ganado vs. Perdido, filterable by period and salesperson.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Leads by Score" icon="chart-bar">
    Distribution of leads across CALIENTE, TIBIO, FRIO, and SPAM buckets for the selected date range.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### 2. Operational Dashboard

Provides a real-time view of work orders, technician capacity, and service throughput.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Open Jobs by Status" icon="list-check">
    Count of work orders grouped by status (Pendiente, En Proceso, En Revisión, Completado) with drill-down by service type.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Technician Workload" icon="users-gear">
    Active job count per technician, highlighting overloaded and underutilized resources.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Average Completion Time" icon="clock">
    Rolling average of job duration from creation to close, segmented by service category and urgency level.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### 3. Financial Dashboard

Monitors revenue recognition, receivables, and cash flow in real time.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Revenue by Period" icon="chart-line">
    Invoiced revenue aggregated by month, quarter, or custom date range. Compared against the same period in prior years.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Outstanding Invoices" icon="file-invoice-dollar">
    Total value of unpaid invoices grouped by aging bucket: 0–30, 31–60, 61–90, and 90+ days past due.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payment Collection Rate" icon="percent">
    Percentage of invoiced value collected within the agreed payment terms.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cash Flow" icon="money-bill-wave">
    Rolling 30-day and 90-day cash flow projection based on issued invoices and expected payment dates.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### 4. Cost Tracking

Compares actual costs incurred on each job against the original estimate to surface budget variances early.

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| Actual vs. Estimated Cost | Per-job comparison of planned cost (from quote) vs. recorded actual cost |
| Parts Cost per Work Order | Breakdown of materials and parts consumption per OT number |
| Labor Cost per Job | Hours logged × labor rate by technician or team |
| Cost Variance % | `(Actual − Estimated) / Estimated × 100` — positive = over budget |

### 5. Profitability Analysis

Calculates gross margin at the job, client, and service category level to identify the most and least profitable areas of the business.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Gross Margin per Job" icon="sack-dollar">
    Revenue from the associated invoice minus total actual costs for the work order.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Gross Margin per Client" icon="building">
    Aggregated margin across all jobs for a given client, ranked by total contribution.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Margin per Service Category" icon="tags">
    Fabricación, Venta, Mantenimiento, and Reparación compared side-by-side to identify high-margin offerings.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Technology

- **Charts**: all visualizations are built with [Recharts](https://recharts.org/), a composable React chart library. Widget types include `BAR`, `LINE`, `KPI_CARD`, and custom composite widgets.
- **Data fetching**: dashboard data is fetched via Next.js Server Actions with full tenant isolation. KPI values are pre-computed and stored in `kpi_history` using configurable formulas in `kpi_formulas`.
- **Widget layout**: each widget's grid position (`position_x`, `position_y`, `width`, `height`) is persisted in `dashboard_widgets` and can be customized per user in `dashboard_preferences`.
- **Immutable history**: the `block_physical_dashboard_delete` trigger prevents physical deletion of any KPI definition, formula, or historical record. Soft delete via `deleted_at` is the only permitted removal path.
